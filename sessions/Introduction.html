Course Overview

Welcome to this transformative journey of leadership development. This course is designed to equip you with biblical principles and practical skills for effective leadership.

Key Scripture Readings

  • Vision and Purpose

    "Where there is no vision, the people perish" - Proverbs 29:18

  • Leadership Character

    "Here is a trustworthy saying: Whoever aspires to be an overseer desires a noble task" - 1 Timothy 3:1

  • Servant Leadership

    "Not so with you. Instead, whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant" - Matthew 20:26

Personal Leadership Inventory

Reflect on Your Leadership Journey

  1. What experiences have shaped your understanding of leadership?
  2. What are your current leadership strengths and areas for growth?
  3. How do you define successful leadership in your context?
  4. What specific leadership challenges are you facing?

Biblical Leadership Principles

Character-Based Leadership

Leadership flows from who you are, not just what you do.

Servant Leadership

True leadership is measured by service and sacrifice.

Vision-Driven Leadership

Leaders see beyond the present and inspire others toward God's purposes.

Transformational Impact

Biblical leadership transforms individuals and communities.

Preparing for the Journey

  • Complete the Personal Leadership Inventory
  • Begin a leadership journal for reflections
  • Find a prayer partner for the journey
  • Review the course schedule and commit to full participation
