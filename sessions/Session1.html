David's Leadership Journey

Key Scripture

"After removing Saul, he made David their king. God testified concerning him: 'I have found David son of Jesse, a man after my own heart; he will do everything I want him to do.'" - Acts 13:22

Character Lessons from David

  • Humble Beginnings

    From shepherd to king - leadership starts in obscurity

  • Heart for God

    Developing intimate relationship with God as foundation

  • Courage Under Pressure

    Facing giants with faith and preparation

Daniel's Leadership Model

Key Scripture

"Now Daniel so distinguished himself among the administrators and the satraps by his exceptional qualities that the king planned to set him over the whole kingdom." - Daniel 6:3

Character Principles from Daniel

  • Excellence in Work

    Setting high standards in all responsibilities

  • Unwavering Integrity

    Maintaining convictions under pressure

  • Wisdom in Leadership

    Combining spiritual insight with practical wisdom

Personal Character Assessment

Self-Reflection Questions

  1. What are your core values and how do they influence your leadership?
  2. Describe a time when your character was tested in leadership. How did you respond?
  3. Which aspects of David's or Daniel's character resonate most with you? Why?
  4. What character qualities do you need to develop further?

Group Discussion Points

  • How does character impact leadership effectiveness?
  • What are the challenges of maintaining integrity in modern leadership?
  • How can we develop David's "heart after God" in today's context?
  • What practical steps can we take to strengthen our character?

Character Development Plan

Create Your Plan

  1. Identify one character quality to develop
  2. List specific actions to strengthen this quality
  3. Set measurable goals for the next 30 days
  4. Find an accountability partner

Weekly Assignment

  • Complete the Character Development Plan
  • Read the story of David (1 Samuel 16-17)
  • Journal daily reflections on character growth
  • Meet with your accountability partner
